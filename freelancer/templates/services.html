Home Loan
What is Home loan ?

A house loan or home loan simply means a sum of money borrowed from a financial institution or bank to purchase a house. Home loans consist of an adjustable or fixed interest rate and payment terms. ... The property is mortgaged to the lender as a security till the repayment of the loan.

Type of Home Loan :
  • Plot Only

    loan for purchase only plot.

  • Construction Only

    Loan for construction of your owned plot.

  • Plot + Construction

    Loan for Plot purchase and construction.

  • House or Flat Purchase

    Loan for purchase of build up house or flat.

Required Documents :
  • KYC
    • Photo
    • Pan Card
    • Aadhar Card\n(for address proof)
    • Process Fee Cheque
    • Bank Statement
  • Salaried
    • Salary Slip
    • Form 16
  • Self Employed
    • Registration Proof
    • ITR with computation
  • Property Documents
    • Agreement to sale (in case of purchase)
    • Property Papers copy (13 year back chain)
    • Map + Estimate (in case of construction)

What is Loan Against Property or Home Equity ?

Loan against property is nothing but a loan which you avail by keeping your commercial/residential property as a collateral. Another name for Loan against property is a secured loan. The security in this kind of loan is the property owned by the person applying for the loan.

What is Vehicle Insurance ?

Vehicle insurance is insurance for cars, trucks, motorcycles, and other road vehicles. Its primary use is to provide financial protection against physical damage or bodily injury resulting from traffic collisions and against liability that could also arise from incidents in a vehicle.
